Reading

Lord Of The Mysteries

Lord of the Mysteries (诡秘之主) is a western fantasy webnovel written by Cuttlefish That Loves Diving (爱潜水的乌贼) and published on Qidian.

I can't express enough how much I love this novel; it's a masterpiece with a plot so rich and dense it could fill an entire football field.

The Perfect Run

The Perfect Run is a book by Maxime J. Durand about Ryan "Quicksave" Romano, an adventurer who can create time loops and redo his life after dying.

The novel's use of time shenanigans is something I personally adore, adding another layer of intrigue to its already well-crafted plot. It's no wonder it stands out as one of the most memorable books I've read.

(Other) Books I'd Recommend!

This section extends the hobbies segment by offering book and web novel recommendations based on my personal experience.

Mother Of Learning

The series follows Zorian, a young mage who is trapped in a time loop, forced to relive the same month over and over again. In Mother of Learning 3, Zorian and his friends race against time to prevent a powerful mage from destroying the world.

Reverend Insanity

Reverend Insanity by Gu Zhen Ren, is a book about Fang Yuan, a person reborn from 500 years in the past with much life experience and wisdom. In the world of Gu with many magical creatures Fang Yuan strives for immortality using any means necessary.

The Legendary Mechanic

The Legendary Mechanic is a Chinese novel series written by Qi Peijia in Chinese language, translate by Atlas Studios, released on webnovel platform. A manga adaptation has been serialized in Webnovel at the same time.

Percy Jackson

Twelve-year-old Percy Jackson is on the most dangerous quest of his life. With the help of a satyr and a daughter of Athena, Percy must journey across the United States to catch a thief who has stolen the original weapon of mass destruction — Zeus' master bolt.

Shadow Slave

Growing up in poverty, Sunny never expected anything good from life. However, even he did not anticipate being chosen by the Nightmare Spell and becoming one of the Awakened - an elite group of people gifted with supernatural powers. Transported into a ruined magical world, he found himself facing against terrible monsters - and other Awakened - in a deadly battle of survival.
